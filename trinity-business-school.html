Trinity Business School

Departments: Trinity Business School (Undergraduate, Masters, MBA, Executive Education, and Doctoral programmes)

Hours: Mon-Fri 8:00 am-10:00 pm, Sat 9:00 am-5:00 pm, Sun Closed (as published on 29 October 2024; bank holidays closed)

Contact: Student Support Services - business@tcd.ie (Trinity Business School, 182 Pearse St, Dublin 2, D02 F6N2)

General Information

Trinity Business School sits between the Naughton Institute and the rugby pitch, with entrances from Pearse Street and from the campus side. The current building was officially opened on 23 May 2019 and was designed as a major teaching and collaboration hub linking Pearse Street to the main campus.

Accessibility Highlights

The school has two public approach routes (Pearse Street and campus-side). For specific access arrangements and event accommodations, students and visitors should confirm details in advance through Trinity Business School support channels and Trinity's Disability Service.

Facilities & Student Services

According to Trinity Estates and Facilities, the building provides lecture theatres, postgraduate office space, smart classrooms, and a rooftop conference room. The central triangular atrium and helical staircase form the main social and circulation space for students and staff.
