Luce Atrium

Departments: Computer Science, Maths, and Physics

Hours: Mon-Fri 08:00 - 22:00 | Sat 09:00 - 13:00

Contact: Hamilton Building Reception

General Information

The Luce Atrium is the buzzing social and academic heart of the Hamilton Building. It serves as a major thoroughfare connecting various science departments and provides students with a bright, open space for meeting and informal study.

Accessibility Highlights

The atrium is fully accessible with level flooring throughout. Multiple automated doors provide entry from the East End of campus, and elevators are available to reach the upper-floor laboratories and offices.

Student Hub Facilities

You’ll find plenty of student lockers, several vending machines, and comfortable social seating areas. It is also the main access point for the MacNeill, Synge, and Joly lecture theatres.
