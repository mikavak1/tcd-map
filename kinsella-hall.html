Kinsella Hall (24/7 Study)

Location: Ussher Library Complex

Hours: 24 Hours (Swipe Access Required)

Facilities: Power outlets, Wi-Fi, Group Study Rooms

General Information

Kinsella Hall is the most popular 24-hour study space at Trinity. Located within the Ussher Library, it provides a mix of silent study desks and more casual seating areas. It is the go-to spot for students during late-night exam seasons.

Accessibility Highlights

Accessible via the main Berkeley Library entrance. Level access and lifts are available to reach all study floors. Dedicated accessible desks with height adjustment are located on the main concourse.
