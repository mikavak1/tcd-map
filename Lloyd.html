Lloyd Institute

Departments: Computer Science Teaching, Mathematics Teaching, IT Laboratories

Hours: Mon–Fri 08:00 – 18:00

Contact: +353 1 896 1520

General Information

The Lloyd Institute is a teaching-focused building near the Pearse Street entrance, mainly used for lectures in computer science, mathematics, and related disciplines. It contains lecture theatres, computer labs, and teaching spaces for large classes.

Accessibility Highlights

The building provides step-free access and lift access between floors. Lecture theatres include accessible seating and accessible toilets are available.
