General Information

Opened in 2022, Printing House Square is a modern, mixed-use development located on Pearse Street. This building represents a significant investment in student services, combining world-class accessibility features with student accommodation, a new Health Service, and the dedicated TCD Disability Service Hub.

Accessibility Highlights

This building is Trinity's premier Disability Hub, built to the highest universal design standards. It features full step-free access from Pearse Street and the main campus. All entrance doors are power-automated.

Lifts serve all floors of the building. The core of the Disability Service is located here, providing accessible meeting rooms, specialized assistive technology labs, and sensory-friendly spaces. Accessible toilets and a Changing Places facility are available.

Facilities & Student Services

The building houses the College Health Service on the second floor. It also provides 249 student undergraduate bed spaces, managed 24/7. Informal study spaces are located throughout the central courtyard area. The Disability Service offers comprehensive supports, assessments, and respite rooms within this hub.
