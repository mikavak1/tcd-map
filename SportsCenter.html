General Information

The Sports Centre is Trinity’s main indoor sports facility, located near the Pearse Street side of campus. It supports a wide range of sports and fitness activities, including gym training, classes, and indoor sports.

Accessibility Highlights

The building provides step-free access, lift access to all floors, and accessible changing rooms. Facilities are designed to accommodate a wide range of users.
