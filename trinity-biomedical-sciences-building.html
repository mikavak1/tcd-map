General Information

The Trinity Biomedical Sciences Building (TBSI) is a multidisciplinary biomedical hub at 152-160 Pearse Street, Dublin 2. Trinity describes it as a cutting-edge facility designed to foster interaction and interdisciplinary research across biomedical science and engineering fields.

Accessibility Highlights

Official accessibility guidance describes the building as fully accessible, with lifts to all floors, accessible toilets, and loop-enabled lecture theatres. Entrance and circulation are planned for step-free movement across teaching and research levels.

Facilities & Student Services

Teaching facilities include large lecture theatres and seminar rooms equipped with advanced AV and video-conferencing, and the institute supports cross-school research infrastructure through shared core technologies and collaborative spaces.
