Biomedical Sciences Institute

Departments: Biochemistry, Immunology, Medicine, and other biomedical sciences

Hours: Department opening times vary; a security/reception desk operates at the main entrance

General Information

The Biomedical Sciences Institute is at 152-160 Pearse Street (Dublin 2) and serves as a major teaching and research location for biomedical disciplines within Trinity. The building includes dedicated student and staff learning spaces and links into wider School of Medicine and biosciences activity.

Accessibility Highlights

Trinity's physical access listing describes the building as fully accessible, with lift access to all floors, accessible toilets, loop systems in all lecture theatres, and accessible seating at both front and rear of theatres. Main access is from Pearse Street.

Facilities & Student Services

The building includes lecture theatres and seminar rooms with advanced AV/video conferencing. Trinity also highlights student spaces such as Zon Mac Leinn at ground level and a student canteen at first-floor level.
